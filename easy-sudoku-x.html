Easy Sudoku X: Beginner-Friendly X-Sudoku Puzzles

Welcome to Easy Sudoku X, the perfect starting point for players new to x-Sudoku or diagonal Sudoku. Unlike classic Sudoku, Sudoku X adds a twist: both main diagonals must also contain all numbers from 1 to 9 exactly once. Play Sudoku X online or print Sudoku puzzle sheets to solve offline at your own pace. These easy puzzles are ideal for improving logic, concentration, and pattern recognition in a fun way.

How to Play Easy Sudoku X

The rules combine classic Sudoku and the diagonal challenge:

  • Every row must contain numbers 1 to 9 without repetition.
  • Every column must contain numbers 1 to 9 without repetition.
  • Each 3x3 block must include all numbers 1 to 9.
  • Both main diagonals must also contain numbers 1 to 9 exactly once.

Beginner-friendly x-Sudoku puzzles help you learn the fundamentals while practicing logic and strategy in a controlled, easy-to-follow way.

Tips and Strategies for Beginners

  • Start with numbers that have only one possible placement in a row, column, block, or diagonal.
  • Use elimination strategies to narrow down possibilities in every section.
  • Look for patterns and repeated numbers across rows, columns, and diagonals.
  • Practice regularly to gradually improve your solving speed and accuracy.
  • Combine online play with printable sheets to develop flexibility in solving techniques.

Additional Advantages of Easy Sudoku X
