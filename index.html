What is Sudoku?

Sudoku is a logic puzzle played on a 9x9 grid, divided into smaller 3x3 boxes. The goal is simple: fill the grid so that each row, column, and box contains all digits from 1 to 9 without repetition. From sudoku puzzles printable to online sudoku free, this game has become a daily mental workout worldwide.
