Medium Samurai Sudoku: The Perfect Balance of Fun and Challenge

Medium Samurai Sudoku is the ideal puzzle level for players who want something more challenging than easy samurai sudoku but less intense than expert grids. Known as Sudoku Samurai or Gattai-5, this fascinating variation combines five overlapping 9x9 sudoku puzzles into one giant interconnected game. With the right mix of logic, patience, and strategy, you can master these puzzles while enjoying hours of entertainment.

How Medium Samurai Sudoku Works

The rules are straightforward yet complex in practice:

  • Each row and column in every grid must contain digits 1 through 9 without repeats.
  • Each 3x3 block must also include all digits exactly once.
  • The overlapping regions connect the central sudoku grid to the outer four, requiring careful coordination to solve.

This balance of rules makes medium samurai sudoku games engaging for intermediate players who want to step up from easy puzzles but are not yet ready for the hardest levels.

Strategies for Solving Medium Samurai Sudoku

Here are some techniques to help you succeed:

  • Start in the central grid since it connects to all four outer puzzles.
  • Focus on overlaps solving one grid can unlock clues for others.
  • Use pencil marks to track possibilities in tricky cells.
  • Look for hidden pairs and triples in overlapping regions to eliminate options.
