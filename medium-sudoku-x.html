Medium Sudoku X: Engaging X-Sudoku Puzzles for Logic Enthusiasts

Medium Sudoku X is designed for players who are ready to move beyond easy puzzles. X-Sudoku, also called x-Sudoku, adds a unique twist to traditional Sudoku by requiring that both main diagonals contain all numbers from 1 to 9 exactly once, in addition to the usual row, column, and 3x3 block rules.
